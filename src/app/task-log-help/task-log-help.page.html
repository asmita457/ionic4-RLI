Enter one task log per client per day. The total hours per day on your task logs must be the same as your total clocked-in time for the day. The following are definitions of the Labor Codes:

MOB: Mobilization or Demobilization

CMD: Command Staff and Chiefs

OPS: Operations Management

LOG: Logistics (Procurement, Transport, Etc.)

FIN: Finance and Administration

CTR: Catering

OTH: Other

CCO: Call Center Operator

TCL: Telcom Crew Lead

YWO: Yard Work Operations
